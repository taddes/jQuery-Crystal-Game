Crystal Game!

A random number will be generated at the start of every game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your crystal score.


You win the game by matching your total crystal score to the random number. You lose if your crystal score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.


Each time the game restarts, the value of the crystals will change.


Good luck!
