. Crystal Game
Crystal Game!

A random number will be generated at the start of every game. It is displayed below.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number and lose if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.


Each time the game restarts, the value of the crystals will change.
